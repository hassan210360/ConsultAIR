Who We Are

Practical Aviation Specialists.
Not Theorists.

Air Consult is a specialized aviation consultancy and operational management company focused on supporting aviation projects across the Middle East, North Africa, and Europe. We are operational people who actually build and run airlines — not consultants with PowerPoint strategies.

Founded in Cairo, Egypt, Air Consult combines deep regulatory knowledge, practical airline management capability, and cargo aviation expertise with established regional relationships across MENA and Europe.

Vision

To become one of the leading aviation consultancy and airline management firms connecting Europe, the Middle East, and Africa through practical aviation solutions and operational excellence.

Mission

To provide airlines, investors, governments, and aviation stakeholders with turnkey aviation solutions — from airline setup and certification to operational management and strategic growth.
